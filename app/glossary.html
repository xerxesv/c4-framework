Arguing
A STRATEGY that can be used in any kind of writing to support a claim with REASONS and EVIDENCE.
Reporting
A writing GENRE that presents information to readers on a subject.
Thesis
A statement that identifies the TOPIC and main point of a piece of writing, giving readers an idea of what the text will cover.
Lorem Ipsum
In publishing and graphic design, lorem ipsum (derived from Latin dolorem ipsum, translated as "pain itself") is a filler text commonly used to demonstrate the graphic elements of a document or visual presentation.
